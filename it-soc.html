SOC Services

24/7 security monitoring, threat detection, and incident response

What We Deliver

Advanced security operations to protect your business from cyber threats.

24/7 Security Monitoring (SIEM)

Continuous monitoring of endpoints, firewalls, servers, and cloud environments for suspicious activity.

Software

Wazuh, ELK Stack, Graylog. Real-time correlation of security events across your environment.

Hardware

SIEM server (4vCPU, 16GB RAM, 500GB SSD) or cloud-based deployment (Azure Canada).

Human Intervention

Certified analysts (CompTIA Security+, CEH) review alerts and validate threats 24/7.

Threat Detection and Intelligence

Proactive identification of malware, ransomware, phishing, and advanced persistent threats (APT).

Software

EDR (CrowdStrike Falcon, SentinelOne), threat intelligence feeds (AlienVault OTX).

Hardware

Endpoints with EDR agents, firewalls with threat prevention modules.

Human Intervention

Analysts investigate IoCs (Indicators of Compromise) and contain threats before escalation.

Incident Investigation and Response

Full lifecycle management of security incidents, from detection to eradication and recovery.

Software

Incident response platforms (TheHive, Cortex), forensic tools (FTK, Autopsy).

Hardware

Secure forensic workstation, write blockers, isolated network for analysis.

Human Intervention

Incident responders follow NIST 800-61 framework: Detection → Analysis → Containment → Eradication → Recovery.

Vulnerability Assessment and Reporting

Regular scanning and reporting of vulnerabilities to ensure compliance and reduce attack surface.

Software

Nessus, OpenVAS, Microsoft Defender for Cloud. Automated scans and prioritization (CVSS).

Hardware

Scanning appliance or cloud-based scanner with access to internal/external networks.

Human Intervention

Security engineers validate findings, recommend patches, and assist with remediation.

Compliance Reporting (GDPR, HIPAA, PIPEDA)

Audit-ready reports for regulatory compliance and executive review.

Software

Custom dashboards in Power BI, automated compliance templates.

Hardware

Secure storage for audit logs and reports (encrypted).

Human Intervention

Security officer prepares reports and presents findings to management.

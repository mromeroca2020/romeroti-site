Operational coverage

Designed for teams that need faster detection, cleaner escalation, and steadier operations

Romanoti's NOC model focuses on operational continuity. It combines monitoring, event handling, reporting, and human review to help organizations detect issues earlier and respond with more discipline.

What we deliver

Core NOC capabilities delivered through a Romanoti operating model

The service is structured around the activities that matter most for availability, operational confidence, and incident readiness.

24/7 Network and Server Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of network devices, servers, and critical services to detect issues before they impact business operations.

Software

Zabbix, PRTG, and Nagios with SNMP/WMI-based monitoring and live dashboards.

Hardware

Firewalls, switches, and servers with SNMP/WMI enabled, plus optional monitoring gateway support.

Human intervention

Certified engineers validate alerts and initiate response for meaningful operational action.

Real-time Alerting and Incident Escalation

Immediate notifications and escalation procedures designed to speed up response when critical events occur.

Software

Email, SMS, Teams, and Slack integrations with threshold tuning to reduce alert fatigue.

Hardware

Mobile devices for on-call engineers and redundant communication paths for critical alerts.

Human intervention

Structured L1/L2/L3 escalation paths with response handling for high-priority events.

Performance Reporting and Capacity Planning

Reporting and trend analysis that help teams plan growth, identify bottlenecks, and improve resource decisions.

Software

Power BI, Zabbix reporting, and PRTG history with dashboards, trends, and forecasting views.

Hardware

Storage for logs and metrics, whether cloud-based or local, with long-term retention support.

Human intervention

Engineers interpret usage patterns and recommend upgrades before operational limits are reached.

Log Management and Analysis

Centralized log collection and analysis to improve troubleshooting, compliance support, and visibility into abnormal events.

Software

ELK Stack, Graylog, and Wazuh with syslog and agent-based aggregation approaches.

Hardware

A lightweight SIEM server or cloud-based deployment sized for log retention and analysis workloads.

Human intervention

Analysts review anomalies, operational patterns, and security-relevant log events.

Incident Response and Resolution Tracking

Full lifecycle tracking from detection to resolution, with status visibility, documentation, and audit-friendly records.

Software

Zammad and Jira Service Management workflows with SLA tracking and ticket history.

Hardware

Secure databases with backup strategies for cloud or on-premises operational records.

Human intervention

Process-guided handling across detection, escalation, resolution, and feedback loops.

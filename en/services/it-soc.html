Email Security Posture

Validation of MX, SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records to reduce spoofing risk, improve domain trust, and strengthen email-related defensive posture.

MX

Proper mail routing and null-MX validation when a domain is not meant to receive email.

SPF

DMARC

Policy validation for none, quarantine, or reject, including reporting alignment.

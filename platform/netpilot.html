Romanoti Platform / NetPilot

Discover and understand your infrastructure.

Romanoti NetPilot is the infrastructure discovery and diagnostics layer of the Romanoti platform. It is designed to help organizations identify devices, services, and technical structure across their environments with greater clarity.

Request information See Ops Assistant

What NetPilot is built for

NetPilot supports visibility, discovery, and diagnostics so teams can move from uncertainty to structured understanding of infrastructure environments.

Discovery Identify network assets and systems.
Services Detect technical exposure and service presence.
Diagnostics Support operational review and investigation.
Clarity Transform infrastructure into usable knowledge.

Why NetPilot matters

Modern environments grow quickly, accumulate complexity, and become harder to understand. NetPilot helps reduce that uncertainty by turning infrastructure discovery into structured visibility.

The problem

Organizations often operate with incomplete visibility into devices, services, and technical relationships inside their environments.

  • Unknown or undocumented devices
  • Unclear service exposure
  • Fragmented operational visibility
  • Slow technical investigation

The NetPilot approach

NetPilot is designed to help technical teams discover infrastructure, understand service presence, and improve the starting point for diagnostics and operational review.

  • Infrastructure discovery
  • Device identification
  • Service detection
  • Diagnostics support

Core capabilities

NetPilot is positioned as the discovery and diagnostics engine that feeds the broader Romanoti platform model.

Network discovery

Help identify systems and technical assets across infrastructure environments.

Device identification

Support visibility into hosts, nodes, and infrastructure components.

Service detection

Reveal active services and technical exposure points for review.

Diagnostics support

Create a stronger base for troubleshooting and technical interpretation.

NetPilot inside the Romanoti platform

NetPilot is not just another standalone tool. It is part of the Romanoti model where infrastructure discovery connects with operational intelligence through Ops Assistant.

NetPilot role

NetPilot focuses on discovering and organizing technical visibility. It is the part of Romanoti that helps answer: what is there?

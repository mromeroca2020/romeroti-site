Prerequisites

To implement our NOC services, the following requirements must be met:

  • ✔
    Network devices and servers with SNMP or WMI enabled: Required for performance and availability monitoring.
  • ✔
    Firewall rules allowing monitoring traffic: Ensure monitoring tools can communicate with your systems securely.
  • ✔
    Contact information for escalation: Up-to-date contacts for after-hours and emergency response.
  • ✔
    Access to critical systems (read-only for monitoring): Secure credentials for monitoring tools.
  • ✔
    Business hours and SLA expectations: Define response times and service level agreements.
